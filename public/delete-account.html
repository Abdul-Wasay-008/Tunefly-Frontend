Delete Your TuneFly Account

Effective Date: October 7, 2025

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your TuneFly account, you can do so directly from within the app or by sending a deletion request via email. Once your request is processed, all personal data associated with your account will be permanently deleted in accordance with our privacy policy.

1. Delete Your Account from Within the App

To delete your TuneFly account from inside the mobile app:

  • Log into your TuneFly account.
  • Go to the Settings page.
  • Click on “Delete my account permanently.”
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted.

2. Request Account Deletion via Email

If you no longer have access to your account or prefer to delete it manually, you can request deletion by emailing us at support@tunefly.io.

Please include the subject line “Account Deletion Request” and provide the email address linked to your TuneFly account. Our support team will process your request within 3–5 business days.

3. What Happens After Deletion

  • All your personal data and account information will be permanently removed from our servers.
  • Your uploaded music, playlists, and preferences will also be deleted.
  • Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.

4. Need Assistance?

If you experience any issues deleting your account or have additional questions, please contact our support team at support@tunefly.io.
