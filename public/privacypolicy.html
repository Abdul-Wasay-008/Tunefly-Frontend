TuneFly Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 7, 2025

Welcome to TuneFly! Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

TuneFly collects minimal information necessary to provide and improve the app’s functionality:

  • User-Provided Information: This includes information you provide when creating an account, such as your name, email address, or any profile details you choose to share.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics (e.g., number of sessions, duration) may be collected to improve app performance. This data does not identify you personally.

We do not collect data from third parties or share your personal information with third-party organizations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality.
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, or support requests.
  • Enhance user experience through minor performance improvements.

3. Sharing Your Information

Your information is never shared, sold, or disclosed to third parties unless required by law or if you explicitly agree to it.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information you’ve shared with us.
  • Request changes to your personal information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@tunefly.io.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Effective Date." Please check this page periodically for updates.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@tunefly.io.

8. How to Delete Your Account

  • Log into your account
  • Go to the Settings page
  • Click on “Delete my account permanently”
  • Confirm the popup by selecting “Delete”
